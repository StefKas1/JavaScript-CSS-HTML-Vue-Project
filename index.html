About me

General

My first name is Stefan (Wilhelm), but you can also call me Stephen, Stephanos, Esteban or click here for more variations of my first name. I am from Germany and I live near the Black Forest (see image above). I have an undergraduate degree in Business Administration. In my free time I enjoy sports. My friends and I regularly go running, cycling, hiking, skiing, and canoeing. Outside of sports, I enjoy reading - and traveling in and outside of Europe. I particularly like cultures and countries that are new and unknown to me, so that I can gain new experiences.

COVID travel

International travel almost came to a standstill during COVID, so I rediscovered exploring within my own region. We went to Lake Constance by bike. We toured through the Black Forest. We have explored the Triberg waterfalls. Hiked over the little mountain Hochfirst. Explored Heidelberg's old town. And I have learned that I do not always have to go far to experience new and interesting things.
